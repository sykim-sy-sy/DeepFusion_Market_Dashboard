# B2B Strategic Partnership & GTM Strategy

## B2B Monetization & Product Lineup
1. **Fusion ECU (HW + SW Bundle)**: For Robotaxi and Defense partners (LIG Nex1). A "Plug-and-Play" module combining DFAI AI with Bosch/Telerian hardware.
2. **Fusion SDK (SaaS/IP License)**: For Tier-1/OEMs with existing compute (NVIDIA, Qualcomm). A platform-agnostic (ECU-agnostic) perception engine.
3. **Evidence Pack (Subscription/Safety-as-a-Service)**: Automated verification reports for global regulations (EU GSR2, Euro NCAP 2026). Generates recurring revenue and creates a high switching cost.
